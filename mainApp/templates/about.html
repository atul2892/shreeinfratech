HEALTH & SAFETY

Our Company has strict safety standards for assuring accident free working at site. A dedicated safety In charge is onsite to make sure that safety rules laid down by Shree Infratech are satisfactory to the client, are strictly adhered to. We use the following basic safety measures for all our works, including many more:

  • No child labour policy is followed and we make sure that our sub-contractors follow the same policy as well.
  • All personnel on site wear helmets and high visibility jackets within work premises. Safety belts are used for all work at heights.
  • First Aid kits are provided at site and staff at site is aware of medical facilities available near the site in case of emergencies.
  • All excavation pits not being worked are barricaded.
  • • Full security setup to make sure that no unauthorized access happens at site.

Provide necessary safety training for all employees.
